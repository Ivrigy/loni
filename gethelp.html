LONI BUT NOT ALONE!



Whether you're facing emergency or an urgent crisis, seeking advice, or simply in need of a listening ear - the right support is just a phone call away. Below, you'll find a curated list of trusted hotlines and helplines offering guidance, support and various solutions to the issue you are facing.




Number against distress: Parents' telephone


If you are worried about your child, feel overwhelmed or simply helpless.


0800 111 0 550


Mon. to Fri. - 9 to 17
Tue. & Thu. - 9 to 19

Helpline-violence against women


Counseling services for women who have experienced or are still experiencing violence. Affected women of all nationalities, with and without disabilities.


08000 116 016


365 days a year
around the clock

Helpline-Violence against Men


For men that have suffered or suffering violence of any kind (mobbing, rape, psychological violence, domestic abuse)


0800 123 99 00


Mon. to Thu. - 8 to 20
Fri. - 8 to 15

Number against distress: children's and youth hotline


The children's and youth hotline for kids, teenagers and young adults who have worries or a problem - no matter the topic.


0800 111 0 550


Mon. to Fri. - 9 to 17
